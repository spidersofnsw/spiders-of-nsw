Sparassidae

Huntsmen spiders are large, often quite hairy arachnids which are commonly found throughout NSW and Australia. These spiders are primarily nocturnal, and will often hide during the day, under bark, rocks and some species will make a retreat out of leaves! Unfourtunatley, these spiders have gained a reputation of being 'unwelcome' guests in cars and homes across Australia. Huntsmen are relatively harmless, with the bite causing mild localised pain, which subsides very quickly.
