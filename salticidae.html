Jumping Spiders

Jumping spiders are by far the most diverse family in the world of spiders. There are giant, hairy and curious jumping spiders, and there are tiny little dancing peacock spiders! Jumping spiders are also the world's smartest spiders. A species from FNQ (Portia fimbriata) uses complex tactics to hunt their prey which ironically is other spiders!
