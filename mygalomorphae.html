Mygalomorphs

Mygalomorphae is the infraorder of many spiders including funnel-web spiders, trapdoor spiders, tarantulas, wishbone spiders and even more! Mygalomorphae is a very diverse infraorder, making it so you can find mygalomorphs almost anywhere. They are common in rainforests, deserts, parks and even backyards! From the infamous funnel-web, to the cryptic mouse spider, Mygalomorphae has it all!
