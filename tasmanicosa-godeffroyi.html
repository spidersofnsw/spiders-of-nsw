The Garden Wolf Spider

Body length:
-Male: 20mm
-Female: 27mm

Tasmanicosa godeffroyi is a species of large wolf spider found in most temeperate reigons of NSW. They make open burrows in dry, eucalyptus forest, but are often encountered in backyards and homes. This species has a painful bite but is relatively harmless to humans, but they are extremely toxic to household pets such as cats and dogs.

The female (top left corner) was found at Windsor Downs Nature Reserve. The burrow (bottom left corner) was found at Webbs Creek NSW.

*IMPORTANT CHARECTARISTICS*

-Has completely black underside.
-Has Union-Jack pattern on carapace.
-Has chevron pattern on abdomen.
