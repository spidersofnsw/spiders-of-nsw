Variable Wolf Spider

Body length:
-Male: 15mm
-Female: 20mm

Venator spenceri is a medium sized wolf spider species which often inhabits dry eucalypt forest. This species makes burrows, around 12-15cm deep, often in hard clay. This species is called the "Variable" wolf spider due to the fact this species can look quite different depending on the habitat/location that the spider was found, although some features remain the same.

The female Venator spenceri (top left corner) was found at Gosford NSW. Male Venator spenceri (top right corner) was found at Parramatta NSW.

*IMPORTANT CHARECTARISTICS*

-Has black patch on the ventral side of the abdomen
-Has a small black patch on or near the bottom of spinerettes
-Has a mild chevron pattern on the abdomen, near the carapace
-Often has a line running through the centre of the carapace
